Run the cheap model. Bongo makes it reliable — and cuts your bill.

You pick your own AI model. Bongo sits in front of it, watches every step, and catches when the model is wrong — even when it fails silently (looks fine, nothing crashes, the answer is just inaccurate). Then it pinpoints what broke and fixes it. This is not a router — you keep your own model; Bongo just makes it accurate.

3Bongo catches the mistakes
It checks every step. When the model is wrong it tells you what broke and fixes it — by escalating that step to a stronger model, or advising how to improve.
